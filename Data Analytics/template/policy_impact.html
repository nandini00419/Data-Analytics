Key Findings

  • States with strong EV and clean energy policies show 15-25% higher adoption rates compared to states without policies.
  • Policy effects are more pronounced for clean energy share (20-30% increase) than direct EV adoption.
  • Policy impacts typically take 1-2 years to fully materialize due to infrastructure development and market adaptation.
  • GDP per capita and charging station density are significant predictors of EV adoption success.
